FAQ

Can I STOP IN OR SHOULD I MAKE AN APPOINTMENT?

Please stop in anytime our office is open. Appointments for boardroom use guarantee availability.

IF THE BOARDROOM IS AVAILABLE, CAN I JUST GO IN AND USE IT?

Yes! Any space not booked can be used by Open Desks Members.

ARE ALL Open Desks MEMBERS SHOWCASED ON THE WEBSITE?

No, only members who have given consent are showcased.

WHAT ARE THE HOURS FOR Open Desks?

Open Desks hours are 8:00 am to 5:00 pm, Monday to Thursday and 8:00 am to 4:00 pm Friday. Closed on Federal and Provincial holidays.

AM I ALLOWED TO BRING MY PET?

Yes, our building is a pet-free zone.
